About Worldway Travel

Everything you need to know

Worldway Travel is your premier destination for all your travel needs from New Zealand and Australia. Established in 1998, we’ve grown into the largest Chinese travel agency in New Zealand, offering international flight tickets, New Zealand domestic tours, and outbound tourism. We are proud to be part of the vibrant New Zealand and global tourism industry.

Our Mission
At Worldway Travel, our mission is to provide the best travel experiences to our customers. Whether you're booking international flights or exploring New Zealand’s beauty, we’re here to support you every step of the way with reliable and personalized services.
Our Commitment
We work with trusted airlines and tour providers to ensure the highest quality experiences for our customers. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to offering exceptional service and ensuring that every trip, whether local or international, is a memorable one. We are proud to actively engage with the travel community and support sustainable tourism practices.
Why Us
We offer comprehensive travel packages, from international flights to expertly guided tours of New Zealand and beyond. Book online from the comfort of your home, enjoy competitive pricing, and benefit from our extensive experience in the travel industry. We guarantee the best value for your travel dollar and peace of mind during every trip.
Expert Assistance
Got a question or need travel advice? Our knowledgeable and friendly team is here to help. Whether you’re planning a local getaway or a multi-destination adventure, our expertise ensures your journey goes smoothly.

Shopping Process

Worldway Travel offers a seamless shopping experience through our website. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you:

  • Browse Products: Search for travel products such as flight tickets, tour packages, and hotel bookings via our easy-to-navigate platform.
  • Add to Cart: Select your desired products and add them to your shopping cart. You can view or edit your cart at any time.
  • Checkout: Proceed to checkout, where you will enter your payment and personal details. All transactions are secure and encrypted for your protection.
  • Order Confirmation: Once payment is completed, you will receive an email confirmation with your booking details.
  • Manage Orders: Log into your account to view or modify your orders as per the applicable terms and conditions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Where can I find detailed product information?

A: You can find detailed descriptions, pricing, and terms & conditions for each product directly on the product page.

Q: Can I modify my shopping cart before checkout?

A: Yes, you can add or remove items from your cart anytime before you finalize your purchase.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers.

Q: Is it possible to cancel my order after payment?

A: Yes, you can cancel your order before it is confirmed. Once a product is confirmed, cancellation through the website is not possible, but you can contact our sales team for assistance.

Q: How can I track my booking status?

A: You can track the status of your purchase or booking through your account dashboard, where all updates will be displayed.

Q: Are there any discounts or special offers?

A: Occasionally, we offer seasonal discounts and promotions. Keep an eye on our website or subscribe to our newsletter for updates.

Q: How do I manage my account?

A: You can log in to your account to update personal information, view order history, and manage your bookings or purchases.

Refund Policy

We understand that circumstances may change, and we strive to offer flexibility. Please review the following refund policies:

{#

#} {# 1. Refund Rules#} {#

#} 1. Refund Rules

Refunds are subject to the terms and conditions of each individual product. Please review these carefully before making a purchase.

2. Cancellation Before Payment

You can cancel your order at any time during the payment process.

3. Cancellation After Payment (Before Second Confirmation)

After payment, if your product or booking has not yet been confirmed for the second time, you may still cancel it.

4. Cancellation After Second Confirmation

Once a product is confirmed for the second time, cancellation through the website is not possible. However, you can still cancel by contacting our sales team via either of the following methods:

  1. Send Message through our online platform
  2. Send Email to our support team at info@worldway.co.nz
5. Refund Processing

Any approved refunds will be issued in the form of credit, which will be applied to your Worldway Travel account for future use.
